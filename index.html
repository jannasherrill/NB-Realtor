    ABOUT

    Nieda Banks moved to Houston, Texas over 19 years ago from Tulsa, Oklahoma and has since called Houston home. Prior to becoming a Realtor she worked in the medical industry as a Surgical Technologist.That experience taught her the importance of being knowledgable, precise and leaving no room for error when it comes to making home buying decisions for her clients.

    Nieda is meticulous and has a keen sense for details. Her positive attitude, patience and integrity are the hallmark of all of her working relationships. Rest assured, with Nieda as your agent you will receive extraordinary service and solid advice regarding all aspects of your real estate experience.

    Her exceptional people skills and outgoing personality add yet another layer of professionalism. She is a skilled negotiator for all of her buyers, sellers, and renters at any stage of their real estate journey.

    Always accessible, Nieda is a team player and has a strong network of colleagues in her circle at Century21 Exclusive Properties. She is part of the Conner Realty Group and they are 1 of the top 10 Real Estate producers in the Houston area.

    In her spare time Nieda enjoys fitness, dancing, decorating and designing her home, cooking and most of all spending time with her two grand daughters, family and friends.

    As an expert in military relocation, I can offer tailored strategies for service members. I specialize in navigating complex processes, and providing sensitive support for families.
